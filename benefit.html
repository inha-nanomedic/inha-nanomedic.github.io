Why join the Inha Nanomedicine Lab? Here's what we offer to graduate students.

What We Offer

Graduate Student Benefits

We invest in our students' growth with comprehensive support spanning financial aid, professional development, and research resources.

🎓
Scholarship & Stipend Support
Financial support including scholarships and a monthly stipend for qualified MS and PhD students. Level of support varies by program and funding source.
💰
Research Funding
Students work on actively funded research projects with resources for materials, lab consumables, and conference attendance covered through project budgets.
🌏
International Conferences
Financial support for presenting research at major international conferences in drug delivery, nanomedicine, and biomaterials.
📝
Publication Support
Mentorship and support for publishing in high-impact international journals. Publication bonuses available.
🏥
Hospital Collaboration
Direct access to Inha University Hospital for translational research and clinical collaboration opportunities.
🤝
Industry Connections
Exposure to the pharmaceutical and biomedical device industries through ongoing collaborative research projects.
🔬
Advanced Equipment Training
Hands-on training with state-of-the-art research equipment and analytical instruments.
📚
Interdisciplinary Learning
Exposure to pharmacy, medicine, biomedical engineering, and materials science through cross-departmental seminars.
🏠
Campus Housing
Priority access to affordable on-campus dormitory housing for graduate students.
Application

How to Apply

Eligibility

  • BS or MS degree holders in pharmacy, chemistry, biology, biotechnology, biomedical engineering, or a closely related discipline in the life and physical sciences.
  • International applicants are welcome.

Contact

Interested students should contact Prof. Su-Geun Yang at sugeun.yang@inha.ac.kr with their CV and a brief statement of research interest.
