Equipment

Our Infrastructure

Core instruments and workstations that power our research programs in nanomedicine, drug delivery, and biomaterial engineering.

⚛
Nanoparticle Synthesis Station
Complete setup for polymer nanoparticle preparation including probe sonicator, rotary evaporator, and magnetic stirring systems.
🔬
Optical & Fluorescence Microscopy
Advanced microscopy suite for cell imaging, nanoparticle characterization, and fluorescence-based assays.
📊
Dynamic Light Scattering (DLS)
Particle size analyzer for measuring hydrodynamic diameter and zeta potential of nanoformulations.
💊
Drug Release Testing System
USP dissolution apparatus with multiple stations for sustained and controlled release profiling.
🧬
Cell Culture Laboratory
BSL-2 certified cell culture facility with CO₂ incubators, laminar flow hoods, and cell counting equipment.
❄
Cold Storage & Lyophilization
Ultra-low temperature freezers (-80°C) and freeze-dryer for sample preservation and powder formulation.
🔥
Thermal Analysis Suite
DSC and TGA instruments for characterizing thermal properties of polymers and drug-polymer interactions.
📐
Rheology & Mechanical Testing
Rheometer for hydrogel characterization, texture analyzer for biomaterial mechanical properties.
