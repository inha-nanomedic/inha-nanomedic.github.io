About Us

Integrated Research Lab for Medicinal Nano and Life Science

Advancing drug delivery, biomaterial engineering, and nanomedicine at Inha University College of Medicine.

Introduction

Healthy Research for Your Health

Our laboratory mainly deals with drug synthesis, medical device design, biomaterial synthesis and preparation to contribute to the medical industry.

We are conducting the challenge of drug delivery, macromolecular oral delivery such as protein and peptide — researching platforms for a new drug delivery system, and ultimately striving to develop patient convenience and advancement in the medical industry.

Located at Jungsuk Bldg B-501, Inha University College of Medicine, Incheon, Korea — we welcome students passionate about biomedicine and nanoscience.

Nanomedicine & Formulation

Photo-responsive nanoparticles, sustained release drug formulation, and protein/peptide drug delivery systems.

Drug Delivery Systems

Microparticle systems, hydrogel platforms, miRNA delivery for cancer therapy, and embolization therapy.

Biomaterial Engineering

Bio-adhesive hydrogels, drug-combined medical devices, polymer coating for stents and restenosis prevention.
